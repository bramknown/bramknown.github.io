Basic Maths Concepts

Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division

  • Addition: a + b (e.g., 2 + 3 = 5)
  • Subtraction: a - b (e.g., 5 - 2 = 3)
  • Multiplication: a × b (e.g., 4 × 3 = 12)
  • Division: a ÷ b (e.g., 12 ÷ 4 = 3)

Negative Numbers

Numbers less than zero, represented with a minus sign (−). Example: −5.

Imaginary Numbers (i, j, k)

  • i is the imaginary unit: i² = -1
  • In engineering, j is used instead of i (j² = -1).
  • In quaternions, i, j, k are used: i² = j² = k² = ijk = -1

Constants and Number Line

  • π (pi) ≈ 3.14159
  • e (Euler's number) ≈ 2.71828
  • φ (golden ratio) ≈ 1.61803
  • √2 ≈ 1.41421

Prime Numbers & Series

Prime numbers: 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17, ...

Fibonacci series: 0, 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, ...
